
T-FRIENDS.COM Party - 18/Sep/2010, 22h, "100 Conversas", Lisbon
Headliner: Daniel Kandi, #87 top DJ Mag

---
“T-FRIENDS” is a new party Project in Portugal, which introduces a new concept for the on how to divulgate specific poorly known music styles: "That every Friend of yours, brings his own Friend!".

The first party will be held in next 18 September in "100 Conversas", Lisbon, and will feature DJ & Producer Daniel Kandi from Denmark, ranked #87 on the dj Mag top 100 - a worldwide poll voted by more than 350.000 persons, from 229  countries.

Also invited is DJ Luismi b2b Chenkio, both of which feature a long experience in similar parties in Spain. The line-up is complete with DJ Estrela (T-Friends co-organizer), and Echo66 of the J4T portuguese forum.

The party will start with a presentation Cocktail & Snacks at 22h, and has live broadcast assured by wavefm.net radio.
